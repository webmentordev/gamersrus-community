Who can ban or unban?

Cheating will not be tolerated. Any violations will result in a permanent ban. There will be no appeals or requests for unbanning from any administrator. All cases will be thoroughly investigated before a decision is made. Proof of the violation and the reason for the ban will be posted on Discord.

Rules

  • ✔ No cheating or exploiting game bugs.
  • ✔ No hacking or use of third-party programs to gain an unfair advantage.
  • ✔ No griefing or intentionally causing harm to other players' bases or structures.
  • ✔ No spamming or excessive use of offensive language in chat.
  • ✔ No sharing personal information of other players.
  • ✔ No scamming or attempting to deceive other players.
  • ✔ No building in or near monuments or public areas.
  • ✔ No blocking or trapping other players in bases or structures.
  • ✔ No racist, sexist, or other discriminatory language or behavior.
  • ✔ Respect all players and staff members.
